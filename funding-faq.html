Who qualifies for business funding?

We work with registered U.S. C-Corps and S-Corps with established operations, business banking, and good credit. You must be in good standing with the Secretary of State.

How much can I receive?

Typically between $50,000 and $500,000 depending on your credit, income, and financial statements.

Can I use a PG to qualify?

Yes. If your business doesn't meet lender credit requirements alone, we match you with a vetted Personal Guarantor.

Do I need collateral?

Some loans are unsecured, especially when backed by a PG. Other options may require basic documentation, but not property or heavy assets.

How fast is funding?

After your intake form is reviewed, most funding decisions are made within 5–10 business days.

Do I pay the PG?

No. We manage payment through escrow — you never pay the PG directly. We handle the legal and financial structure.

Can startups apply?

Only if you're registered and generating revenue. This program is not for side hustles or informal businesses.

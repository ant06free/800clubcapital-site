Step-by-Step Process

  • Step 1: Apply online through our secure form — no credit check required.
  • Step 2: Get matched with a seasoned credit card tradeline based on your profile.
  • Step 3: You’re added as an Authorized User (AU) to that card. You won’t receive or use the physical card.
  • Step 4: In 2–4 weeks, the tradeline appears on your credit report.
  • Step 5: You remain on the card for 60–90 days to benefit from the history and limit.
Apply for the Credit Builder Program

Why This Works

Authorized User tradelines can help improve your credit score by increasing your average account age, available credit, and positive payment history — all key factors in credit scoring models.

Who It's For

This program is for individuals looking to boost their credit profile to qualify for funding, mortgages, or better rates on loans — with no obligation to manage a physical card or make payments.

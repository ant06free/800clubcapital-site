How It Works

Authorized User Credit Building Program

Step-by-Step Process

  • Step 1: Apply Online
    Submit your details using our secure intake form. No credit check is required.
  • Step 2: Match with a Tradeline
    We match you with an aged credit card account based on your credit goals.
  • Step 3: Get Added as an Authorized User (AU)
    Your name is added to the tradeline, allowing the payment history to appear on your credit report.
  • Step 4: Watch Your Credit Improve
    In 2–4 weeks, most clients see a positive change to their credit report.
  • Step 5: Stay On for 60–90 Days
    You remain an AU for the agreed time to benefit from the tradeline's age and payment history.

Become a Personal Guarantor

How It Works

  1. Apply using our secure form — takes less than 2 minutes.
  2. We review your credit profile to ensure you meet our 800 Club standards.
  3. You’re matched with a qualified, vetted business in need of a PG.
  4. Once approved and funded, you get paid up to $10,000 via escrow.
  5. After 12 months, your name is removed as PG — no long-term obligation.

Minimum Requirements

  • FICO Score: 750+
  • Utilization under 30%
  • 5+ open tradelines
  • No late payments, collections, bankruptcies, or derogatory marks
  • Less than 3 inquiries per bureau
  • Over $10K in total available credit/loans
  • Annual income of $70,000 or more

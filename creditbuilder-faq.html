What is the Credit Builder Program?

You are added as an Authorized User (AU) on a seasoned tradeline. This can improve your credit score by increasing your account age, available credit, and payment history.

Do I get a credit card?

No. You will not receive or use the card. This is purely a credit-building strategy — no purchases, no card access.

Is there a credit check?

No hard credit pull is required. We do a soft check or internal review to match you to the right tradeline.

Will this hurt my score?

No. This adds a positive tradeline with strong history. It does not add debt, new balances, or affect utilization.

How long does it take to show on my report?

It typically appears within 2–4 weeks after being added to the card.

How long do I stay on the tradeline?

Most AU placements last 60–90 days. After that, you're removed automatically or by request.

Can I do this more than once?

Yes! Many clients cycle through multiple tradelines to build a more robust profile.

What are the ideal results?

Better credit utilization, longer average age of accounts, improved approval odds for funding, mortgages, and credit cards.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about becoming a Personal Guarantor with 800 Club Capital

1. What is the 800 Club?

800 Club Capital connects individuals with strong credit profiles (FICO 750+) to vetted businesses seeking personal guarantors (PGs) to unlock funding. PGs are compensated up to $10,000 and legally removed after 12 months.

2. What does a Personal Guarantor (PG) do?

You lend your credit profile — not your money — to help a business secure funding. You are paid and removed after 12 months. You are not involved in day-to-day operations.

3. Is this risky?

Very low risk. We structure the deal with legal contracts and escrow. PGs are paid first before the business can access more than 10% of funding. PG removal is guaranteed in writing after 12 months.

4. Will this affect my credit score?

No. A soft inquiry or a temporary hard pull may occur, but you will not be liable for the business's debt or usage. Your utilization and payment history remain unaffected.

5. How much do I get paid?

Up to $10,000 per funded deal. Payment is made via escrow within 5–10 days of approval and disbursement.

6. Who qualifies to be a PG?

  • 750+ FICO score
  • Same name across all bureaus
  • 5+ tradelines
  • <30% credit utilization
  • No derogatories
  • <3 inquiries per bureau
  • $10K+ credit/loans available
  • $70K+ annual income

7. How is the business matched with me?

We screen each business for creditworthiness, background, and legitimacy before introducing a PG. We only match you with low-risk opportunities.

8. What legal protection do I have?

  • PG Agreement (outlines role, payout, removal)
  • Escrow Instruction Agreement
  • 12-month removal clause
  • UCC or indemnity protections if needed

9. Do I need to invest money?

No. You are not investing any money. You are compensated for lending your credit profile, not your capital.

10. What happens if the business defaults?

We pre-structure protections. You’re paid first, and the business must hold insurance and a repayment plan. If needed, legal action will be taken to protect the PG.

11. How often can I be a PG?

You can be a PG for up to 2 deals per year, depending on your credit profile and history.

12. Can I refer someone?

Yes! You earn up to $1,000 for every qualified PG or business you refer that completes a deal.

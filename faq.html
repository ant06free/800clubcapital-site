Frequently Asked Questions

Is this program legal and secure?

Yes. We work within legal frameworks, use contracts, and partner with licensed attorneys and escrow services to ensure all parties are protected.

How does a Personal Guarantor (PG) earn money?

Once your credit profile is matched with a vetted business and the funding is approved, you are paid up to $10,000 via escrow.

Do I need to pay anything up front?

No. There is no upfront fee required to participate as a PG. The process is free to apply and be evaluated.

Will this affect my credit?

Your credit report may show an inquiry, but there are no late payments, new lines, or revolving balances involved. Risk is minimal.

Is my name removed after 12 months?

We work with legal counsel to ensure you are formally released from your PG obligation once the timeline or terms are fulfilled.

What if the business defaults?

Funds are managed through escrow, and the business cannot access more than 10% of the funds until you are paid. Legal safeguards are in place to protect you.

Is this a loan or debt?

No. Acting as a personal guarantor does not mean you are borrowing or repaying money. You are not responsible for payments unless a default occurs under the agreement.

Who can apply to be a PG?

Anyone with a 750+ FICO score, low utilization, clean credit history, and strong financials may qualify. We screen all applicants thoroughly.

What types of businesses are supported?

We only support registered U.S.-based C-Corporations that pass vetting. No sole proprietors or individuals.

How long does it take to match with a business?

It can take 1–4 weeks depending on your credit profile and availability of vetted businesses seeking a PG.

Why do you need people with 750+ credit scores?

Businesses often need a personal guarantor to strengthen their application for funding. Lenders are more likely to approve applications backed by a strong credit profile.

How soon will I get paid?

Once the business receives funding and all agreements are signed, you receive your payment — usually within 7–14 days via escrow.

Can I do this more than once?

Yes. If your initial PG transaction is successful, you may be eligible to act as a guarantor again for future businesses — increasing your total earnings.
